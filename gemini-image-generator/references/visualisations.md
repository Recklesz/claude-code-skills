### 4. Common Component Recipes
If you need to visualize specific data types, use these pre-tested descriptions:

*   **For Comparisons (e.g., 8.4 vs 9):**
    *   *"Two vertical bars side-by-side. The main bar is solid Vibrant Teal. The target bar is outlined in grey or lighter teal."*
*   **For Percentages (e.g., 39%):**
    *   *"A crisp, flat-style circular progress ring in Vibrant Teal. No glow effects. Text placed clearly in the center."*
*   **For Increases (e.g., +17%):**
    *   *"A small, crisp Success Green (#2A7D5F) upward arrow icon placed next to the percentage."*
*   **For Time/Volume (e.g., Minutes):**
    *   *"A minimalist, flat-style 3D icon of [clock/hourglass] in soft Teal. Non-intrusive."*

### 5. Pro-Tip for Google Vids
If the AI keeps spelling words wrong or if you have complex text (like "Partner: Dave, It's Not What You Think"):

1.  **Simplify the Prompt:** Ask the AI to generate the *layout* and the *numbers* only.
2.  **Use Overlays:** Use the Google Vids **Text Tool** to add the complex headers or partner names on top of the generated image. This ensures perfect spelling and sharpness.
